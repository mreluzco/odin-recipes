Cheesecake Brownies

Brownies marbled with a cheesecake topping.

This recipe spices up your out-of-the-box brownie mix with an easy cheesecake topping. All told, this recipie should take about 40 minutes for 24 brownies. The directions for making these brownies are based on a Betty Crocker Fudge Brownie mix; follow instructions on box for brownies if using a different brand.

Ingredients

Brownies

  • 1 (18.25 oz) package of brownie mix
  • 3 tablespoons water
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 large eggs

Cheesecake Topping

  • 1 (8 ounce) package of cream cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/3 cup white sugar

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Farenheit. Grease a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Prepare brownie mix: Stir brownie mix, water, oil, and eggs in a medium bowl until well blended. Spread evenly into the prepared pan.
  3. Prepare cheesecake topping: Beat together cream cheese, egg, and sugar using an electric mixer until smooth. Dollop the cream cheese mixture on top of the brownie batter. Swirl together using a knife or skewer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven for 22 to 25 minutes. Brownies will be done when a toothpick inserted comes out clean. Cool in the pan, cut into squares, and serve.
